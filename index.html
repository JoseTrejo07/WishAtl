Why choose us?

WISH ATL, Atlanta's own global streetwear fashion house, has brought the world's most cutting edge labels in designer streetwear to Atlanta for two decades. Founder Lauren Amos' focus has always been to find unique talent, tell compelling stories, and to shine a light on new artists and designers.

WISH ATL Flagship store is based in the historical Carnegie Library building in Little Five Points, Atlanta. For the past 20 years Amos has created the most tantalizing customer experience to the local & global community. The diversity of brands and sophistication of WISH ATL became like no other streetwear store, and quickly gained national notoriety

Customer Reviews

Really dope lifestyle clothing store in the little five points area of Atlanta. It features fashion wears and accessories on the ground floor ranging from high end lifestyle brands & niche market labels to local independent designers. On the bottom level is a shoe gallery with a wide range of styles, brands, and collectibles to satisfy many a sneaker head and or style chaser.... the design and presentation of the sneakers is pretty awesome so its worth just a look even if you don't plan on picking anything up. Additionally they also have a gallery space 2 doors down that features really strong artwork by various Atlanta artists.

Killer Mike

The Parking: There is a free parking lot behind the building. This parking lot is also known for its ICONIC Outkast mural.

The Atmosphere: Wish has an upstairs and downstairs. Upstairs has apparel, books, & other accessories. Downstairs is where all of the sneakers reside.

Overall, if you're into sneakers, or just want to do some shopping on the new hotness, you should definitely check out Wish ATL.
